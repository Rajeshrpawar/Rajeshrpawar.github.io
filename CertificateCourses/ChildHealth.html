Certificate Course in Child Health

The goal of this course in Child Health is to produce a competent skill developed doctor who recognizes the health needs of children.

On completion of this course, the candidate will be able to:

• Develop the basic clinical skills of history taking, clinical examination and case presentation in Child Health.

• Obtain knowledge of the common clinical problems seen in daily practice.

• Understand the need for multidisciplinary management of sick newborns as well as pediatric patients.

Fee Schedule : ₹ 5,350

Course Duration : 2 Months

Syllabus

  Topics

• Common problems in new born

• Common OPD illness

• Neonatal resuscitation with demonstration

• Normal care of preterm babies and their feeding (Demo of tube feeding)

• Breast Feeding

• Gastroenteritis and its management

• Febrile convulsion and its treatment

• Meningitis and its management

• Fever for more than 7 days

• Jaundice

• Vaccines

• Deficiencies in growing babies (Iron, Calcium, Multivitamin)

• Measles Mumps, Rubella, Chickenpox.

• Pediatric gastroenterology, herpetology and nutrition

• Pediatric immunology, infectious disease and allergy

• Pediatric intensive care medicine

• Respiratory disease

• Pediatric emergencies

• Investigations

• Common drugs used in pediatrics

FAQ'S

  Q. What is the course design and study method?

This is a 2 months weekend program. Lecture videos by our expert doctors will be available on our website, which can be accessed at your convenient time of your course duration.

   Q. What is the eligibility?

Any Medical Graduate can enroll for the Course like MBBS/ BHMS/ BAMS/ BUMS etc.

   Q. How do I get practical training?

For Certificate programs, there is no practical training, only lectures will be provided.

If you are looking for practical training, then you can register for Adv. Diploma courses.

  Q. Is this course recognized by any University/ Statutory Board?

The course is for knowledge and skill development, and is not affiliated to any University or Statutory Board.

   Q. What is the examination pattern?

The final exam will be conducted through Online MCQs.

   Q. What is the minimum passing percentage?

50% is the minimum passing percentage.

   Q. What if i fail or not appeared ?

If you have failed an exam or did not appear, don't worry. You will get one free chance to reappear for the exam. However, if you fail or do not appear in the first free attempt, a fee f Rs.500/- per subject will be charged for any subsequent attempts

   Q. Is Hostel facility available?

No. Hostel facility is not available for the Certificate program. The free Hostel facility is available only for Advanced Diploma Courses.

  Q. How to get registered for the course?

  • Click on the Enroll Now button above and you will be directed to the Application Form page.
  • Fill in all the required details and upload your education documents.
  • Make the fee payment by clicking the Pay Now button on the Application Form page.
  • You can make the online fee payment through debit/credit card and net banking options.
  • Once you submit the form, education documents, and course fee, you will receive a confirmation email from our side.

    • Please Note: We only accept online filled application forms and online payment. There is no other mode of enrolling into our courses.

       Q. Is there any Stipend given during posting at the hospital?

    No. Stipend will be given to certificate courses. Stipend will be given to Advanced Diploma courses only, from the second month of posting, depending upon the students work performance.

       Q. What if I have to cancel my registration?

    Fees once paid will not be refunded/ adjusted or transferred.

       Q. What happens next once I enroll in the course?

    As soon as you enroll yourself, you will get an email from our side on your registered email id stating the confirmation of your admission. After confirmation, within the stipulated time period you will receive a welcome email containing your login credentials of the course with important dates. You can proceed with studies as per the course commencement dates.

       Q. After passing, how do I get my Certificate?

    After the successful completion of exam and formal procedures, you will get the Certificate at your registered postal address.
