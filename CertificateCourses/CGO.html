Certificate Course in Gynecology and Obstetrics (CGO)

CGO is aimed at practitioners who have gained basic skills in non-complex obstetric procedures &gynaecology and is designed to facilitate a better understanding of reproductive disorders and pregnancy issues, to be able to provide a planned care.

On completion of this course, the candidate will be able to:

• Develop the basic clinical skills of history taking, clinical examination and case presentation in Obs. & Gynaec.

• Obtain knowledge of the common clinical problems in Obs. & Gynaec.

• Understand physiology of basic complications and conditions in Obs. & Gynaec.

• Understand the principles and methods of fertility control.
